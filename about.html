About me

My name is Roland Opeyemi and I am currently a Graduate student at Austin Peay State University pursuing a masters degree in Computer Science, where I take various courses in Database Management, Web Development and Web Design.

Prior to starting my Masters Degree, I worked as a Front-End Engineer at an Education Technology company where I started as an Intern, then moved to leading the Design Team where I was focused on developing user-friendly interfaces that are compatible and responsive to microsites, mobile applications and personal computers utilizing HTML5, CSS, JavaScript and custom Amazon services like Elastic Beanstalk and EC2 (Elastic Cloud Compute). On the other hand, I was focused on driving sales for the Prodigy School Management Software as well as using data to propose innovative solutions which helped us achieve reduction in operation cost, increase in revenue and customer acquisition and retention

Part of the things I achieved here was being able to develop new ideas and recommendations on features to be added to the LMS Platform. I was also able to get the software across to over 300 schools in Nigeria and Ghana, which included high schools and private universities with 40,000 students as active users. And the software was also made available and accessible to schools for students with disabilities across Ghana and Nigeria.
